Five html tags that I have learned:

Superscript tag:
The superscript tag is a tag that will apply a superscript to text within the tag.
Abbreviation tag:
The abbreviation tag will display the abbreviation of certain text when your mouse hovers over it.
Emphasis tag:
The emphasis tag is used to emphasize important text. It will make the text look different.
Section tag:
The section tag groups content that is related, together.
Nav tag:
The nav tag is where all the links to other pages will be contained.
