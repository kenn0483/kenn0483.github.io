About me

Hello, my name is Eamonn Kennedy. I am 20 years old. This is my second year at Algonquin College, last year I was in the IAWD program. I switched to the IMD program which I started January 12th 2021. I have experience using Final Cut Pro and Adobe Illustrator. One thing I learned in the Internet Applications and Web Development program was how to query a database.

I enjoy playing the french horn and I have played in several orchestras in Ottawa. I have played the french horn for around 8 years. My other hobbies include such things as mountain biking in the Greenbelt and Gatineau Park, and stargazing, although I wish Ottawa had less light pollution so I could see more stars.
