About

Eamonn Kennedy - Horn Player

Hello, my name is Eamonn Kennedy. I have been playing the French horn for the past 8 years. I picked up the horn when I was in 7th grade. For a career, I wish to play the French Horn. If I can’t get a job related to the horn, I will look for something else in the field of music. When I first started the horn, I joined the Ottawa Youth Orchestra Academy and have made my way up all the way to The Ottawa Youth Orchestra. Most recently, I have played with the Ottawa Pops Orchestra. Over the years, I have had two major teachers. These include Elizabeth Simpson and Debra Laroque, and more. I am now currently studying with Elizabeth Simpson over Skype. Once this pandemic is over, I hope to get back to ensemble playing.

My Mission

My life’s mission is to hopefully become a professional horn player. If possible, I hope to one day play the French horn in a professional orchestra. To achieve this, I practice the horn every single day. I also get bi-weekly Skype lessons from my teacher, Elizabeth Simpson. I have experience playing many different genres of music from Baroque to classical, and also pops music like Star Wars and Harry Potter soundtracks. I am constantly learning new things. I hope one day to put all my skills to the test and to become a professional.

My Vision

In the future, I see myself as a professional French Horn player. I see myself playing with various professional orchestras. Including hopefully, the National Arts Centre Orchestra here in Ottawa. Playing the French horn has always been a lifelong ambition of mine. I am determined to do what ever it takes to successfully meet my goals. I will do my very best to achieve them using which ever methods I happen to use.
