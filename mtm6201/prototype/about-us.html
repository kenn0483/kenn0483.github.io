ABOUT US

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide mothers and their chidren with a large variety of ice-cream flavours to choose from depending on their mood. Wether your feeling happy and cheerful, or sad and depressed, we have got the perfect flavour for you. In the future, we wish to release our own seperate product line to attract dads.

Our Vision

Our Vision for the future is to provide our customers with the highest quality and best tasting ice-cream in the world that will fit thier moods. We want to see a smile on the face of every one of our customers when they eat our ice-cream. We want to ensure that our customers are satisfied with our products. We can feel happy for them and marvel at the success of all our hard work.
